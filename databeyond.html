UBC BizTech

UBC BizTech is the University of British Columbia’s largest premier technology club with over 1000 passionate members. Winning the 2022 ‘AMS Best Academic Club Award’ and ‘CUS Most Innovative Club Award,’ UBC BizTech continues to push the boundaries of Business and Technology through fresh engaging experiences.

From October 2022 - April 2023, I served at UBC BizTech as a Director of Design.

Data & Beyond

Data & Beyond by UBC BizTech x BOLT UBC is a half-day conference to guide students through the journey of discovering the prospects of data science-related careers. As a Director of Design, I was in charge of developing a comprehensive visual identity for the conference to reach 2600+ followers on social media and 100+ partners/ sponsors, including Apple, Google, Microsoft, Meta, Amazon, Quora, and many more leading tech companies. This was our second-largest conference of the year after the renowned UBC Blueprint.

The goal of the design is to deliver a contemporary, captivating experience that attracts tech enthusiasts and highlights our dedication to excellence.

Theme

I went with neumorphism with a high color contrast to ensure accessibility yet still keep the clean, minimalistic aesthetics. The design elements include neumorphic components, 2 purple-to-mint gradient options, and references to color palettes of both UBC BizTech and BOLT UBC.

Logo

After a lot of sketches on my own and brainstorming with the team (shoutout to the very supportive Maddie Nguyen, Rora Vongsombath, Karen Agustino, and the rest of BizTech's Design - Marketing team), the final logo for Data & Beyond is a typographic design that incorporates a fun twist: the infinity sign linking the letters "b" and "e."

This detail conveys the key message we want to communicate to our audience: the emergence of data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence is propelling us towards an infinite future.
