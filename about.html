Just a Note

Thank you for making the time to visit my portfolio. Now that you are here, before rambling about all the items I’m listing on my resume, I want to create a tiny space to write about my favourite word in Vietnamese, thương.

Being a Vietnamese is a big part of who I am - the more I get to go out to experience the world, the more connected I feel to the Vietnamese identity. I grew up speaking and loving tiếng Việt as well as all the literature and poetry that are core who we are. In my free time, I am trying to get back to writing - an item on my bucket list is to have something actually published (my wildest dream!)

There isn’t a completely precise translation of the word thương in English - the closest would be "love," but not necessarily in a romantic sense. "Yêu" is strong, passionate, heated love, while thương is about acceptance, empathy, and stability. I think a lot about the values that the word thương carries and how those translate to what I want to do at work.

After a while in software development, I came to the realization that I care deeply about designing great user experience and products that somehow help a lot of people. I truly believe through product design we can build a better world, one pixel at a time.

I'm always down to chat, so don't hesitate to drop a note :)
