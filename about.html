A Note on "Thương"

Thank you for making the time to visit my portfolio. Now that you are here, before rambling about all the items I’m listing on my resume, let me take this time to tell you about my favourite word in Vietnamese - "thương."

Being a Vietnamese is a big part of who I am - the more I get to go out to experience the world, the more connected I feel to the Vietnamese identity. I grew up speaking and loving tiếng Việt as well as all the literature and poetry that are deep in our DNA. In my free time, I am trying to get back to writing - an item on my bucket list is to have something actually published (my wildest dream!)

There isn’t a completely precise translation of the word "thương" in English - the closest would be "love." "Yêu" is strong, passionate, heated love, while "thương" is about acceptance, empathy, deep care, stability, and true understanding. I think a lot about the values that the word "thương" carries and how those translate to what I want to do at work.

After a while in software development, I came to the realization that I care deeply about designing great user experience and meaningful products that somehow help a lot of people. I truly believe through product design we can build a better world, one pixel at a time.
